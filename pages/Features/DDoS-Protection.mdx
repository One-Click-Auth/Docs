# Implementing DDoS Protection with TrustAuthX 🛡️🌐

In the digital world, safeguarding your platform against malicious traffic is crucial. TrustAuthX's DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) Protection feature allows you to implement robust DDoS protection measures within your platform.

This feature helps to ensure uninterrupted service availability by protecting your platform against DDoS attacks. While TrustAuthX is immune to DDoS attacks by default, if you're using a custom domain, you might want to consider enabling this feature.


<br/>
<br/>

## Steps to Implement DDoS Protection in TrustAuthX
<br/>
<br/>
<Image src="/bot-detection.gif" alt="TrustAuthX DDoS Protection" width={1000} height={1000} />

<Steps>

<br/>
### Step 1: Navigate to the Organizations Tab 🗂️

Start by signing up at TrustAuthX and creating a new organization. Then, click the **"Manage"** button in the Organization table for the required organization.

### Step 2: Access the "Settings" and Select the "Security" Tab ⚙️

Next, select **"Settings"** from the sidebar and then choose the "Security" tab. Here, you'll see the different security Security and their features.

### Step 3: Enable "DDoS Protection" 🛡️

Click the toggle button in the "DDoS Protection" section. A popup will appear, informing you that this feature can be turned on at any time, but can only be turned off 14 days after activation.

### Step 4: Confirm Your Selection ✅

Finally, click "Yes" to confirm. This will add the DDoS Protection to your platform and all resources will be allocated to your organization. Please note that this is a paid feature.

</Steps>

This document provides a step-by-step guide on how to implement DDoS Protection with TrustAuthX, safeguarding your platform against malicious traffic and ensuring uninterrupted service availability. 🌐💻
