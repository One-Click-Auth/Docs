# Enabling Social Sign-On with TrustAuthX 🌐🔑

In the digital world, providing a convenient and streamlined user experience is crucial. TrustAuthX's Social Sign-On feature allows you to enable social sign-on functionality within your platform.

This feature allows users to authenticate and access your platform using their social media accounts. Social sign-on simplifies the registration and login process, enhancing user convenience and streamlining the onboarding experience. One of the key advantages of TrustAuthX is that you can integrate as many social connectors as you want at once, offering users a wide range of options for authentication.

<br/>
### Step 1: Navigate to the Organizations Tab 🗂️

Start by signing up at TrustAuthX and creating a new organization. Then, click the **"Manage"** button in the Organization table for the required organization.

### Step 2: Access the "Settings" and Select the "Services" Tab ⚙️

Next, select **"Settings"** from the sidebar and then choose the "Services" tab. Here, you'll see the different authentication Services and their features.

### Step 3: Enable "Social Sign-On" 🌐🔑

Click the toggle button in the "Social Sign-On" section. A popup will appear, informing you that this feature can be turned on at any time, but can only be turned off 14 days after activation.

### Step 4: Confirm Your Selection ✅

Finally, click "Yes" to confirm. This will add the Social Sign-On to your platform and all resources will be allocated to your organization.
