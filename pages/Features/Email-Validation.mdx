# Implementing Email Validation with TrustAuthX 📧✔️

In the digital world, ensuring the accuracy and legitimacy of user-provided email addresses is crucial. TrustAuthX's Email Validation feature allows you to implement robust email validation within your platform.

This feature helps to mitigate the risk of accepting invalid or fake email addresses, improving the overall quality of user data and communication. This not only enhances the reliability of your user base but also ensures effective communication.

<br/>
<br/>

## Steps to Implement Email Validation in TrustAuthX
<br/>
<br/>
<Image src="/email-validation.gif" alt="TrustAuthX Email Validation" width={1000} height={1000} />

<Steps>

<br/>
### Step 1: Navigate to the Organizations Tab 🗂️

Start by signing up at TrustAuthX and creating a new organization. Then, click the **"Manage"** button in the Organization table for the required organization.

### Step 2: Access the "Settings" and Select the "Methods" Tab ⚙️

Next, select **"Settings"** from the sidebar and then choose the "Methods" tab. Here, you'll see the different authentication methods and their features.

### Step 3: Enable "Email Validation" 📧✔️

Click the toggle button in the "Email Validation" section. A popup will appear, informing you that this feature can be turned on at any time, but can only be turned off 14 days after activation.

### Step 4: Confirm Your Selection ✅

Finally, click "Yes" to confirm. This will add the Email Validation to your authentication flow and all resources will be allocated to your organization. This feature is absolutely free!

</Steps>
